Creative Process

From initial concept to final artwork

01

Ideation

Exploring narratives, gathering references, and developing the core concept through sketches and mood boards.

02

Sketching

Creating rough thumbnails and refined line art to establish composition, form, and character personality.

03

Rendering

Applying colors, lighting, and textures with attention to atmospheric depth and semi-realistic details.

04

Finalization

Refining details, adjusting values, and adding final touches to achieve a polished, cohesive artwork.
